Sansi

Sansi is a mysterious spirit who wanders through the shadows of an ancient monastic apothecary. There, he learned the secrets of an ancient order of alchemist monks from Turin. At the end of his long apprenticeship, the order entrusted him with the guardianship of 39 rare and precious herbs, essential for the preparation of an ancient and legendary distillate.
